About the Herero People

The Herero people are a Bantu ethnic group primarily found in Namibia, with significant populations also found in Botswana. They are known for their resilience, rich traditions, and unique cultural identity.

The Herero culture is strongly connected to cattle farming, traditional dress, and community values. Their history is an important part of Southern African heritage.
