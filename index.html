Who Are The Herero People?

The Herero people are a Bantu ethnic group primarily found in Namibia, Southern Africa with significant populations also found in Botswana and Angola. They are known for their resilience, rich traditions, and unique cultural identity. The Herero culture is strongly connected to cattle farming, traditional dress, and community values. Their history is an important part of Southern African heritage.
