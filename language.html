Learn Otjiherero

Otjiherero is the language of the Herero people.To help preserve and promote the language I will introduces basic greetings, numbers and common expressions.

Learn Basic Otjiherero

Greetings

Otjiherero English
Moro! Hello
Mua pindoka nawa? How are you?
Nawa. I am fine.
Ndangi. Thank you.

Numbers 1-5

  • 1 -Imwe
  • 2 -Mbari
  • 3 -Ndatu
  • 4 -Ine
  • 5 -Ndano

Family Words

  • Mother -Mamaa
  • Father -Tate
  • Child -Omuatje
